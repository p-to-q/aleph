# Support

Use the smallest public surface that fits the request:

- Bug: open a bug issue with reproduction steps.
- Question: open a question issue or discussion.
- Feature: open a feature request with the user problem and acceptance signal.
- Security: follow `SECURITY.md`, not public issues.
- Governance or workflow change: open a governance note.

Maintainers may close issues that are support requests without reproduction, duplicates, or outside the project scope.
